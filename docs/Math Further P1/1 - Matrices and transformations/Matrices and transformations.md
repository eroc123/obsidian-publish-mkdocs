A matrix (matrices) is simply an array of numbers and is usually written inside curved brackets It is denoted by capital letters, often in bond e.g. $\text{A} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 3 & -7 \\ 4 & 0 & 5 \end{pmatrix}$
